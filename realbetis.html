Story

Real Betis Balompié ( Spanish : Real Betis Balompié ) is a football club from Seville , Spain Founded in 1907 . The team is currently playing in the first division of the Spanish League . Its stadium is Benito Villamarin, the number of seats is 52,700. The main colors of the team are green and white . The club is known as the slogan of its fans: “Real Betis lived even if they lost.” In Spanish (Andalusian dialect): “viva er beti manque pierda”. Betis , after the river that passes through the city of Seville, according to the Latin name, green is a symbol of peace and white is a symbol of hope .Derived from the banner of the Umayyad Caliphate in Cordoba

Luis Oliver is a businessman, who previously owned the clubs of Jerez and Cartagena, despite all the opposition to his taking over Betis, he bought Betis for 16 million euros by Manuel Ruiz de Lopera. As president of Betis, his most important projects were to balance the Betis budget, and his most prominent steps were to get rid of the player with high salaries
